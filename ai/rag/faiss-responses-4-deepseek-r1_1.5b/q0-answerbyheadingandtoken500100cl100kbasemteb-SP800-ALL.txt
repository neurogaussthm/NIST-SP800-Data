NIST's role is to provide guidelines that ensure agencies meet necessary security requirements, offering recommendations such as data protection measures, encryption, and access control. While these documents may not apply exclusively to national security, they are crucial for broader agency operations aiming to safeguard sensitive information under FISMA and related regulations. The goal is to ensure compliance with security standards while addressing potential threats across federal systems.
