5. **Simplifies Compliance and Auditing**: With least privilege, administrators can more easily track who has what permissions and audit changes in user access, making compliance with regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, or PCI DSS easier and more efficient.

6. **Reduces Human Error Risk**: By limiting users' privileges to only those operations required for their roles, there's less opportunity for human error leading to unauthorized actions or data breaches due to misconfigurations or accidental changes.
